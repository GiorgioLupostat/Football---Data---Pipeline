## 📈 Sample Analytics Insight

By plotting **Expected Goals per 90** against **Expected Assists per 90**, the model segments players into distinct performance quadrants:

* **Top-Right Quadrant (The Elite Tier):** Players dominating both shot volume and creation. Highly indicative of top-flight readiness.
* **High xA / Low xG (The Playmakers):** Invaluable profiles for teams lacking progressive passing and final-third creativity.
* **High xG / Low xA (The Target Outlets):** High-efficiency finishers capitalizing on penalty-box positioning.
